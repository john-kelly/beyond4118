Beyond 41.18 is a community-based online counter-mapping platform on which users submit their personal experiences living and organizing in 41.18 zones. The genealogy of 41.18 must be understood through the lens of those who are unhoused. Through story-telling and counter-archiving, we seek to start conversations and bring attention to patterns of lived experiences in Los Angeles. View map!
